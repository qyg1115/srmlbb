🛠 Customer Support

🙋🏻‍♂ Can I change my team name after registration?

Yes, please contact us and we will assist with the procedure. Once the event begins, team names and player info cannot be changed.

🙋🏻‍♂ Are there any restrictions on hero picks or skins?

No restrictions, as long as the team includes a Jungler, Marksman, Mage, Roamer, and Fighter.

🙋🏻‍♂ Can a player join more than one team?

No, each player may only register with one team.
